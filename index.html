Public Safety

Police Patrol Zone Optimization

Plano Police Department, TX

Goal & Methodology

Optimizing patrol zones to balance officer workload across 38,374 crash records. Applied NKDCE to mitigate spatial bias and used p-median models to delineate zones.

Outcome

Delivered a data-driven framework ensuring equitable service coverage for 6, 11, 13, and 21 patrol car operations.
